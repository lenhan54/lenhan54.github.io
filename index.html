Welcome to Nhan Digital Studio.

We provide digital content management tools that help users create, organize, and publish marketing content on Pinterest.

About Our Application

Our application connects with Pinterest through official Pinterest APIs to help authorized users manage their Pinterest content.

The application supports content organization, board management, and publishing of user-created marketing materials related to products, brands, and online business activities.

How It Works

Users connect their Pinterest account through Pinterest OAuth authentication. The application only accesses Pinterest information after the user grants permission.

Users maintain full control of their Pinterest account and can revoke access at any time through Pinterest account settings.

Data Protection

We respect user privacy and only use authorized Pinterest data for providing content management features.

We do not sell, rent, or share user information with third parties.
