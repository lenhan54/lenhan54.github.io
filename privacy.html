Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Nhan Digital Studio respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

Application Purpose

Nhan Digital Studio is a Pinterest content management application designed to help authorized users create, organize, and publish content on Pinterest.

Information We Access

When users connect their Pinterest account, the application may access information provided through Pinterest API, including:

  • Pinterest account information
  • Boards information
  • Pin information

Access is only granted after the user completes Pinterest OAuth authorization.

How We Use Information

The information accessed through Pinterest API is used only to provide content management features, including organizing boards and publishing content requested by the authorized user.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share user information with third parties.

Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information and only retain data necessary to provide application functionality.

User Control and Data Deletion

Users may disconnect the application from their Pinterest account at any time.
